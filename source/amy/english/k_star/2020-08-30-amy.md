## 4.Daily talk 口语对话练习

```sample
1)
What’s your name?
My name is ______. / I am_____.

2)
How old are you?
I am _____ years old.

3)
How are you?
I’m good/ great/ wonderful, ... thank you.

4)
What’s this?/ What’s that?
It’s a/an _____.

5)  
What color is it?
It is/ It’s _____.

6)   
Is it _____?
Yes, it is./ No, it isn’t.

7)   
What color are they?
They are/ They’re _____.

8)  
Are they _____?
Yes, they are./ No, they aren’t.
```
